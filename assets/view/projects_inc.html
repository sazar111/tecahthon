RULES
Team Event
Eligibility: II, III Years (CSE & CSBS).

Description:
Do you love making projects? Do you have an arsenal of projects that you've made? Well, if you answered yes to any of the above, this event is for you. Create your own project and pitch it to our panel just like a startup enterpreneur.

This event includes a hands-on workshop where you will learn all the necessary skills to participate in the contest.

Team Size:

  • Teams must consist of a minimum of 2 participants and a maximum of 3 participants.
  • The team members should be of the same batch.
  • All team members must be from the same year.

BATCH-A:
Monday--Wednesday--Friday

BATCH-B:
Tuesday--Thursday--Saturday


ROUND 1: Selection Round

  • Participants can choose their own problem statements
  • The overall project idea, abstract and the problem statement is to be presented to the judging team.
  • Selection Parameters: Idea presented, Technological Stack used, Approach in forming the solution

ROUND 2: Final Round

  • The participants will have to pitch the idea in front of the judging panel.
  • The judging team will judge the winners based on:
    • How far is the project developed?
    • The approach used for solving the problem
    • Presentation of the working model
    • Marketing Metrics (USP and Explanation of Marketability)
